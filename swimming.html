Swimming: the art of moving through water

    The art of swimming

    Swimming is a fantastic full-body workout that combines cardiovascular exercise with strength training. It's low-impact, making it suitable for people of all ages and fitness levels. The feeling of weightlessness in the water can be incredibly relaxing, while the challenge of improving your technique and speed keeps it engaging. Whether you're doing laps for fitness or enjoying a leisurely swim, it's a great way to stay active and cool off.

    Story line

    I vividly remember starting my swimming journey at the age of 12. Since then, I have had the incredible opportunity to swim in various beautiful locations, including the serene waters of the Vietnamese sea, the stunning Russian Black sea, and the refreshing pools in Korea. Each experience has brought me a sense of relaxation and tranquility, allowing me to focus on my breathing and feel completely at one with the water, as if nothing else exists but me and the gentle waves surrounding me.
