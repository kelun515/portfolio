Music: The Universal Language

    • My Musical Journey

      Music has been an integral part of my life, allowing me to express myself creatively and connect with others who share my passion. As a solo guitarist in a rock band, I've had the opportunity to explore various musical styles and push my boundaries as a musician.

    • MANAN

      MANAN is my first musical band, and I had the honor of being the solo guitarist. Our talented ensemble included a skilled pianist, a dynamic drummer, another guitarist, and three incredible singers, all under the guidance of our dedicated coach, Nikita. We all came together at Mon Russ Joint High School, where our shared passion for music forged lasting friendships and unforgettable memories.

    • Our first concert

      Our first concert took place in June 2024, and it was an unforgettable experience. A massive crowd gathered to watch us perform, filling the venue with excitement and energy. The atmosphere was electric as we took the stage, and the support from our friends, family, and fans made the night truly special.
