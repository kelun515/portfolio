Taekwondo: the art of self self-defense

      Taekwondo benefits

      I have been training in taekwondo for five years, and throughout this journey, I have experienced remarkable improvements in my stamina and overall health. My self-defense skills have significantly increased, providing me with greater confidence. Additionally, my flexibility has improved, allowing me to execute techniques with greater ease and precision.

      Taekwondo club

      Our Taekwondo club, the Taekwondo Development Center, is based in Mongolia and has become a second home for me. Within our club, I have met many friendly individuals who possess impressive taekwondo skills. The supportive atmosphere fosters camaraderie, and we constantly motivate each other to improve and excel in our training.

      Competition

      My hardest and most important competition took place in 2023 at the Korea open in Chuncheon. This massive international event brought together talented athletes from around the world, making the competition fierce. I was thrilled to earn a bronze medal, which not only recognized my hard work but also motivated me to continue improving in taekwondo.
